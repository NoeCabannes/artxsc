Literature for everyone

Literary classics adapted for children and adults with ADHD, thanks to AI designed by education professionals.

Rediscover your child as a reader

No more screens! Art × Science aims to create eye-catching, captivating content, while maintaining the original writing style.

0% net profit

Art × Science is a non-profit association under the French law of 1901. Every penny is re-invested in the search for quality materialized content made in France, more accessible than ever.

Huge catalog
The plan is to adapt 4,500 books in 3 languages, 5 times more than the current English-language commercial alternatives.
Adapted visual organization
Shorter chapters, visual story support (plot elements, character relationships) and special formatting are designed to avoid mental overload.
Multi-sensory elements
Benefit from books that simultaneously engage several senses to increase information retention.
Non-linear, interest-based reading
Restructured to accommodate the non-linear thinking common in neuro-atypical profiles, with flexible reading paths that are explicit about the plot to attract curiosity, but also allow for scrolling without losing the narrative.

Roadmap
Milestones

01. First steps
Open member/volunteer registrations, obtain public interest association status (France).
02. Research & Development
Research and development of the “Sapere aude” project, its artificial intelligence under strict quality control.
03. Early distribution
Journalists/education professionals on the waiting list and major supporters will receive the first copies.
04. Global launch
Official launch of the online catalog featuring over 3,500 titles, with gradual rollout in bookstores.
