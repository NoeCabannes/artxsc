Huge catalog
The plan is to adapt 4,500 books in 3 languages, 5 times more than the current English-language commercial alternatives.
Adapted visual organization
Shorter chapters, visual story support (plot elements, character relationships) and special formatting are designed to avoid mental overload.
Multi-sensory elements
Benefit from books that simultaneously engage several senses to increase information retention.
Non-linear, interest-based reading
Restructured to accommodate the non-linear thinking common in neuro-atypical profiles, with flexible reading paths that are explicit about the plot to attract curiosity, but also allow for scrolling without losing the narrative.
